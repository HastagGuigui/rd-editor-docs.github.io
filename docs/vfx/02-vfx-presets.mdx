# VFX Presets

## Silhouettes on Heartbeat

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/vfx/presets/silhouettes.gif')} alt="Silouettes on Heartbeat preset" width="85%"/>

Cycles through row tints on each pulse.

## Vignette / Vignette Flicker

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/vfx/presets/vignette.png')} alt="Vignette preset" width="85%"/>

Darkens the edges of the screen.

## Colorful Shockwaves

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/vfx/presets/shockwaves.gif')} alt="Colorful Shockwaves preset" width="85%"/>

Changes the hue of everything in the room on a heartbeat shockwave (after a hit).

## Bass Drop on Hit

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/vfx/presets/bass_drop.gif')} alt="Bass Drop on Hit preset" width="85%"/>

The Bass Drop effect but only activates on a hit.

## Shake on Heartbeat / Shake on Hit

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/vfx/presets/shake.gif')} alt="Shake on Heartbeat preset" width="85%"/>

The row shakes slightly on each pulse or the hit, depending on which event you choose.

## Wavy Rows

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/vfx/presets/wavy_rows.gif')} alt="Wavy Rows preset" width="85%"/>

Rows move up and down in a room.

## Screen Tile

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/vfx/presets/screen_tile.png')} alt="Screen Tile preset" width="85%"/>

Splits the room into 2x2, 3x3, 4x4, or a custom sized grid.

## Vertical Light Strip

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/vfx/presets/vertical_light_strip.gif')} alt="Vertical Light Strip preset" width="85%"/>

Makes a vertical strip of light around the current beat, and darkens everything else.

## Screen Scroll (+ VHS)

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/vfx/presets/screen_scroll_vhs.gif')} alt="Screen Scroll (+ VHS) preset" width="85%"/>

Scrolls the screen horizontally or vertically, and can also have the flickery VHS lines as an option. Custom Screen Scroll does not have a VHS option.

## Row Tints

These events are a limited form of the *Tint Rows* event, but can be used for a quick tint depending on what you need. These events are used when placing presets.

## Cutscene Mode

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/vfx/presets/cutscene.png')} alt="Cutscene Mode preset" width="85%"/>

Horizontal black bars appear around the screen. Removes the arms until Cutscene Mode is disabled.

## Blackout / Noise / Glitch Obstruction

Completely turn the room black, into static, or the glitchy background from the Boss 1 stage.

## Rain

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/vfx/presets/rain.png')} alt="Rain preset" width="85%"/>

Adds a rain effect. Will occasionally cause the background to flash white.

## Matrix

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/vfx/presets/matrix.png')} alt="Matrix preset" width="85%"/>

Like the *Matrix* preset, but can be used more easily.

## Falling Petals

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/vfx/presets/petals.png')} alt="Falling Petals preset" width="85%"/>

Pink or snow blue petals will slowly fall from the top of the room.

## Snow

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/vfx/presets/snow.png')} alt="Snow preset" width="85%"/>

Adds a snow effect that will fall from the top of the room.

## Bloom

<figure className="no-side-margins">
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/vfx/presets/bloom.png')} alt="Bloom preset in Blink 182" width="85%"/>
	<figcaption>Blink 182</figcaption>
</figure>

Causes light from bright sources to bleed into the surrounding room, works well with custom backgrounds.

## Hall of Mirrors

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/vfx/presets/hall_of_mirrors.png')} alt="Hall of Mirrors preset" width="85%"/>

Very complicated. Skip to the Hall of Mirrors page to get an explanation of its effects.

## Greyscale / Sepia

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/vfx/presets/greyscale_sepia.png')} alt="Greyscale and Sepia presets" width="85%"/>

Applies a monochrome or sepia filter to the selected room.

## JPEG Distortion

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/vfx/presets/jpeg_distortion.png')} alt="JPEG Distortion preset" width="85%"/>

Glitchy blocks appear that shift small sections of the room left and right.
